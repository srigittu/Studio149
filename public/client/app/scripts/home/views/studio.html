Founder's Note
Fashion is for everyone and it is a way of expressing yourself. We, at Studio 149, believe in making timeless outfits that will be value additions to your wardrobe. With special attention to details like cuts, proportions, choice of fabric, & embellishment, our clothes are a fine mix of aesthetic and functionality. 

Studio 149 is an ever evolving label headed by a team of young and vibrant designers having diverese interests and credentials in the field of fashion. We work along with an amazing team of craftsmen to create unique hand-crafted outfits that are a perfect complement to every changing season in fashion, making sure we always formulate for our clients, a bold fashion statement .
- Swathi Purushothaman

Come Visit
You can get in touch with us via mail or whatsapp with your requirements or references and our team of designers will get back to you shortly with our ideas .

This way we can work together on creating your dream outfit. After discussing with you our design team will come up with design sketches and suggestions for fabrics to be used.

The approximate budget will be quoted as well and after the client’s approval the order will be taken ahead for production on receipt of 70% of the advance payment. The balance needs to be paid at the time of final fitting or before dispatch (in case of outsation orders).
Every person has a unique taste and we at Studio 149 would love to work along with you to create your dream outfit. Send us an enquiry with your requirements and our designers will be in touch with you shortly.
